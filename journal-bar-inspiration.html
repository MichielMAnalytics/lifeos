Journal bar — inspiration

LifeOS · home

The current journal block is too tall and carries visual weight of a full card. We want something smaller, that still surfaces today’s priorities and wins, but compresses like a strip — something you glance at, not sit in. Below: nine variants pulled from productivity apps I liked the density of.

Each variant below is rendered with the same mock data so you can compare directly. All use dark-theme LifeOS tokens.

Variant 1 — Daily intention strip

One line. One intention. Nothing else.

inspired by · Sunsama

Sunsama’s daily planning ritual puts a single sentence at the top of the day. Everything else is below the fold. If MIT / P1 / P2 are already in the priorities checklist section, the journal strip could just carry the narrative intention and nothing more.

Which one?

My picks, ranked: V8 (ticker) if you just want to shrink, V1 (intention) if you’re willing to move MIT/P1/P2 down into the priorities checklist section (they’re already there), V4 (big date) if you want the home page to feel more emotional in the morning. V3 (weekly) is the most ambitious — not a “smaller” change but a different mental model.
